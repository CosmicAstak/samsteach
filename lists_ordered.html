How to Boil an Egg

  1. Put eggs in a pot filled with cold water
  2. Bring the water to boil
  3. Take the pot off the heat, cover it, and let it sit for 12 minutes
  4. Remove the eggs from the water and cool them by running water over them or placing them in a bowl of ice water to cool off
  5. Peel and eat
